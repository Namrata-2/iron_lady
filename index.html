About Iron Lady

Empowering women to break barriers and reach the pinnacle of leadership

Elevating One Million Women to the Top

Iron Lady is on a transformative mission to break the glass ceiling that has limited women's advancement in corporate leadership. We believe that every woman possesses the innate potential to lead, innovate, and excel at the highest levels of business.

Through our revolutionary Business War Tactics methodology, we equip women with the strategic mindset, practical skills, and unwavering confidence needed to navigate complex corporate landscapes and secure C-suite positions.

1M+ Women to Elevate
100% Commitment
50+ Countries

From Vision to Reality

Iron Lady was born from a simple yet powerful observation: while women represent 50% of the workforce, they hold less than 25% of senior leadership positions globally. Our founder, after experiencing firsthand the challenges and biases that women face in corporate environments, decided to create a solution that would systematically address these barriers.

2018

The Beginning

Started with a small group of 10 women in a Bangalore coworking space, sharing experiences and strategies for career advancement.

2020

First Success Stories

Our first batch of graduates achieved remarkable success, with 80% securing promotions within 6 months of completing our program.

2022

Scaling Impact

Expanded to online format, reaching women across India and internationally. Launched our flagship C-Suite League program.

2024

Global Movement

Now a thriving community of 1000+ women leaders, with alumni in Fortune 500 companies and successful entrepreneurs.
